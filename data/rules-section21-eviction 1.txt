If you get a section 21 notice, it’s the first step your landlord has to take to make you leave your home. You won’t have to leave your home straight away. If your section 21 notice is valid, your landlord will need to go to court to evict you. You might be able to challenge your eviction and stay longer in your home. You might have to pay court costs if you decide to challenge your eviction. You should make sure you have a good case before you decide to go to court.
